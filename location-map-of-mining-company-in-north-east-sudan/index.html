Location map of mining company in North East Sudan

I created this map by integrating Sudan’s national highway network with my digitized track records of unpaved roads frequently used to access the Hamisana area. Hamisana is a major artisanal and small-scale mining (ASM) market located in the Red Sea State, northeast Sudan. This work highlights key access routes and provides valuable insights for understanding transportation dynamics in this significant mining region.

I used ArcMap 10.2 to digitise the roads, using PBS to import the Bing satellite images.
